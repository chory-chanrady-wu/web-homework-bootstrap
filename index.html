FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions...

1. How do I place an order on your website?

Simply browse the products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Enter your shipping information and select a payment method to complete the order.

2. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, digital wallets, and Cash on Delivery (COD) for selected locations.

3. How long does shipping take?

Shipping usually takes 2–5 business days depending on your location. International orders may take 7–14 days.

4. Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email or SMS to monitor your delivery status.

5. What is your return and refund policy?

You can return any product within 7 days of delivery. Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days after we receive the item.

6. Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to selected countries. Shipping charges may vary based on your region.

7. How do I change or cancel my order?

Orders can be changed or canceled within the first hour after purchase. Contact our support team immediately if you need help.

8. How do I create an account?

Click the “Sign Up” button at the top of the page and fill in the required information to create your account.

9. Are my payment details secure?

Yes, all transactions are encrypted using industry-standard SSL security to ensure your personal and payment information remains safe.

10. Do you offer cash on delivery?

Yes, Cash on Delivery is available for selected cities and products.
